Pizza Recipe

How to Make Pizza

Makin delicious pizza like Mako pizza can be fund and easy. Follow this simple recipe and get deliciousness at the end.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Dry Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Basil
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Make the garlic oil, then brush it onto the grill grates.
  3. Shape the dough and place it on the hot grill.
  4. Flip the crust and brush with oil.
  5. Top the pizza however you like, close the lid, and cook until the cheese melts.
